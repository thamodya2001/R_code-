## 📌 KRSS: Kernel Regression Approach for Non-Parametric Two-way ANOVA : A Novel Approach

Our team developed **Kernel Ridge Regression-based Statistical Significance Testing (KRSS)** as a robust non-parametric alternative to traditional two-way ANOVA, 
which often fails due to strict assumptions such as normality and homogeneity of variances. KRSS utilizes **Gaussian kernels** and **permutation testing** to capture main and interaction effects without relying on these assumptions.

Simulation studies and real-world applications show that KRSS performs comparably to classical ANOVA while remaining highly reliable under non-normal and heteroscedastic conditions. It is particularly effective for moderate sample sizes, making it a flexible and practical approach for analyzing complex factorial designs.
